Josefin Slab was the first typeface designed by Santiago Orozco. It follows the 1930s trend for geometric typefaces and is meant to be between Kabel and Memphis, but with modern details. The designer wanted to stick to the idea of Scandinavian style, so they put a lot of attention into the diacritics. It has some typewriter attribute inspired by Letter Gothic.

Body Copy

Nestled in a sun-dappled corner of an ancient estate, the light academia garden exudes an air of tranquility and intellectual charm. The pathways, lined with weathered stone, meander through a lush tapestry of greenery. Ivy climbs the weathered walls of a nearby study, where windows are framed by soft, white curtains that flutter gently in the breeze. The garden’s design encourages exploration and contemplation, with hidden nooks that invite moments of reflection or quiet reading.

A vibrant assortment of plants flourishes throughout the space. Delicate lavender sways gently, filling the air with a calming fragrance that mingles with the sweet notes of blooming jasmine. Towering sunflowers stand as cheerful sentinels, their bright faces turned toward the sun, while lush ferns create a soft carpet of green in shaded corners. Each plant seems to tell a story, from the resilient daisies that nod in the wind to the stately rosebushes bursting with layers of soft petals in various shades of pink and cream.

Amidst the flora, antique furniture adds a touch of character to the garden. A weathered wooden bench, adorned with soft cushions, provides the perfect spot for visitors to pause and indulge in a favorite book or sketch the enchanting surroundings. A small, wrought-iron table holds an assortment of well-thumbed volumes and a delicate teapot, inviting leisurely afternoons of tea and conversation. The garden feels like a sanctuary for the mind, where creativity can flourish among the blooms and whispers of nature.

As dusk approaches, the garden transforms into a magical realm. Soft lanterns flicker to life, casting a warm glow on the path and illuminating the delicate petals of night-blooming flowers. The soft chirping of crickets and the gentle rustle of leaves create a symphony of sounds that enhance the garden’s serene atmosphere. Under a canopy of stars, this light academia garden becomes a haven for dreamers and thinkers alike, where nature and knowledge intertwine in perfect harmony.

Whimsy in the Garden

An Afternoon of Light Academia

Saturday, May 18th 2 PM - 10 PM

Step into a world of enchantment at Whimsy in the Garden, an afternoon dedicated to the serene beauty of light academia. Set in a lush, sun-drenched garden, this gathering invites you to explore a realm where nature, creativity, and whimsicality intertwine. Attendees are encouraged to dress in soft pastels and vintage-inspired attire, immersing themselves in the charming atmosphere of the day.

The afternoon will feature a delightful array of activities, including a botanical treasure hunt where participants will discover hidden literary quotes among the plants, each leading to small prizes. An inviting sketching nook will allow budding artists to capture the garden's beauty, while a cozy poetry corner will host readings from classic and contemporary poets, encouraging guests to share their own verses inspired by nature.

As the sun begins to set, the garden will be adorned with twinkling fairy lights, transforming it into a magical haven. Guests can indulge in a curated selection of herbal teas and delectable pastries from a vintage tea cart, creating an ideal setting for leisurely conversations. With gentle melodies from a string quartet playing softly in the background, Whimsy in the Garden promises an afternoon of connection, creativity, and inspiration, where the beauty of plants and the spirit of light academia come alive.

Literary Blooms

A Garden of Words

Saturday, April 20th 2 PM - 5 PM

Join us for Literary Blooms: A Garden of Words, a delightful afternoon that celebrates the harmonious blend of literature, plants, and the enchanting spirit of light academia. Set in a lush garden adorned with blooming flowers and towering trees, this event invites book lovers and nature enthusiasts alike to revel in the beauty of both worlds.

As guests arrive, they will be welcomed with a botanical cocktail or herbal tea inspired by famous literary works. The afternoon will feature a series of engaging activities, including a "Book and Bloom" exchange, where attendees can bring a favorite book and a small potted plant to swap with others, creating a personal library of green companions.

Throughout the garden, cozy reading nooks will be set up with blankets and cushions, encouraging guests to curl up with a book from our curated selection of classic and contemporary light academia favorites. A designated "Poetry Path" will invite participants to contribute their own lines or favorite verses on wooden plaques, creating a living anthology inspired by nature.

As the event winds down, guests can gather for a relaxed discussion about their favorite books and the inspiration they draw from the natural world. With the gentle rustle of leaves and the sweet fragrance of flowers in the air, Literary Blooms promises an afternoon filled with the joys of reading, the beauty of nature, and the connections formed through shared passions.
